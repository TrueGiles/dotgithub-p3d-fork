## How can I play?
The game requires FAT filesystem support to load assets at runtime. Currently, we only support:

- **[melonDS](https://melonds.kuribo64.net/downloads.php)** (multiplatform emulator)
- **DS / DSi / 3DS hardware** via [TWiLight Menu++](https://wiki.ds-homebrew.com/twilightmenu/)

See the **[README](https://github.com/p3d-project/persona-3-dual/blob/main/docs/README.md#running-the-game)** for the latest information, & access the latest releases **[HERE](https://github.com/p3d-project/persona-3-dual/releases)**

# Common Q&As
## What about FEMC / The Answer / P4?
At this moment our full attention goes to developing the **base, male route** of Persona 3 Dual and currently there are *no plans to develop anything else*. The most we will do for the foreseeable future would be a palette and **model swap for FEMC**.
## When will it be finished?
Unfortunately, our crystal ball broke so we can not make an accurate prediction, however it will be a matter of a **couple of years**. Feel free to *check out the [GitHub page](https://github.com/p3d-project/persona-3-dual) for new releases* to stay up to date though.
